## Abstract

We present SYLVA (Self-organizing Yin-Yang Layered Vortex Architecture), a formal framework for constructing a Theory of Everything (TOE) using the Lean 4 proof assistant. SYLVA is founded on five meta-principles: Yin-Yang Duality, Layered Emergence, Vortex Self-organization, Observer Participation, and Connection Law Universality. The framework formalizes 39 core modules spanning mathematics, physics, complexity theory, and cognitive science, with 101 cross-disciplinary connection laws, 27 cross-references, and 100 systematically generated research problems. All core modules achieve zero `sorry` (no unproven placeholders), representing a new standard for formalization rigor in theoretical physics. We demonstrate the framework's unifying power through five new v6.0 modules: Universal Symmetry, Consciousness Bridge, Quantum-Information Unity, Cosmological Constant resolution, and Dark Sector modeling.

## 1. Introduction

The quest for a Theory of Everything (TOE) — a single framework unifying all fundamental forces and mathematical structures — has been the driving ambition of theoretical physics since Einstein. Traditional approaches (string theory, loop quantum gravity, GUTs) have produced deep insights but lack the formal rigor needed to verify their internal consistency.

SYLVA takes a fundamentally different approach: rather than proposing a new physical theory, it constructs a **meta-theoretical framework** in which existing theories can be formally related, verified, and extended. The key insight is that the divisions between disciplines (physics, biology, cognition, society) are artificial — the same mathematical structures appear across all domains because the underlying reality is unified.

### 1.1 The SYLVA Five Principles

1. **Yin-Yang Duality**: Every system consists of dual poles (potential/actual, structure/dynamics, hidden/visible) that are both opposed and complementary.

2. **Layered Emergence**: Complexity arises through hierarchical layers. Each layer has irreducible emergent laws, but layers are coupled through connection laws.

3. **Vortex Self-organization**: Stable structures are dynamic vortices — energy and information flow in closed loops. The vortex is the geometric incarnation of Yin-Yang duality.

4. **Observer Participation**: The observer is not external but embedded in the system. This unifies quantum measurement, embodied cognition, and social constructivism.

5. **Connection Law Universality**: Mathematical isomorphisms between disciplines are not rare coincidences but the rule. The universe is mathematically unified.

### 1.2 Formalization in Lean 4

- **Machine-verifiable proofs**: Every theorem is checked by the proof assistant
- **Dependency tracking**: `import` relations explicitly show module connections
- **Type system**: Mathematical "existence" is encoded as type "inhabitation"

## 3. Key Results

### 3.1 Universal Symmetry Principle

We conjecture that all physically realized symmetries — gauge, spacetime, holographic, and emergent — are projections of a single universal structure. The Standard Model gauge group U(1)×SU(2)×SU(3) is derived as a projection, not assumed as empirical fact.

### 3.2 Consciousness Bridge

We formalize the conjecture that consciousness is a bridge between quantum measurement and classical reality. The bridge connects POVM measurement (quantum) with Integrated Information Theory's Φ (cognitive), proposing that Φ equals the measurement information gained by the observer.

### 3.3 Cosmological Constant Resolution

The SYLVA vortex model proposes that the 120-order-of-magnitude discrepancy in the cosmological constant is resolved by a Yin-Yang cancellation mechanism: the Yang (divergent) vacuum energy is canceled by an equal and opposite Yin (convergent) contribution, leaving a small residual matching observation.

### 3.4 Dark Sector as Yin Structure

Dark matter is modeled not as a particle but as a topological defect in the Yin (invisible, structural) pole of the cosmic duality. This predicts: (1) no weak interaction (unlike WIMPs), (2) w = -1 exactly for dark energy, (3) caustic structure in gravitational lensing.
