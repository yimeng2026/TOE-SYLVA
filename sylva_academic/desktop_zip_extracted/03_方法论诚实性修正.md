## 修正任务1：数值结果表述（§4.2）

### 原文引用

> "The baseline simulation achieves agreement at the 5–6% level without free parameters beyond the network topology. Fine-tuning of γ and κ brings the result to within 0.1%."

### 修正理由

1. **"without free parameters"是误导性表述**：γ（幂律指数）、C（聚类系数）、κ（曲率-耦合常数）本身就是模型的自由输入参数。声称"除网络拓扑外没有自由参数"相当于说"除模型中的所有参数外没有自由参数"，这是语义游戏。

2. **从+0.7%到0.0%是过拟合而非预测**：数据表显示，仅将γ从3.0微调到2.9（一个约3%的参数调整），配合C从0.3调到0.4和引入κ=0.15，就能把误差从+0.7%压到0.0%。这是在5个参数组合中挑选最优者（γ=2.9, C=0.4, κ=0.15），属于明显的后验调参（post-hoc tuning），而非先验预测。

3. **5-6%误差在粒子物理标准下不构成"一致"**：在粒子物理中，精细结构常数α的测量精度达10^-10量级（通过Josephson效应）或10^-9量级（通过量子霍尔效应）。5-6%的相对误差相当于约10^7倍标准偏差。即使在模型比较的标准下，5-6%的误差也只能说明"定性趋势正确"，绝非"定量一致"。

4. **混淆"预测"与"敏感性分析"**：baseline结果（γ=3.0, C=0.3）的+0.7%误差具有偶然性——如果改变γ到2.5或3.5，误差就从-2.1%跳到+8.9%。这说明结果高度参数依赖，不能被视为独立预测。

## 修正任务2：形式化状态表（Formalization Status）

### 原文引用

> "Zero sorry is verified" / "All core physical content is formalized"

### 修正理由

1. **"Zero sorry"是技术性障眼法**：在Lean等定理证明器中，一个`postulate`或`axiom`不计为`sorry`，但它代表一个**未证明的假设**——在科学方法论上与`sorry`等价。将15个物理假设包装成`postulate`然后声称"zero sorry"，类似于把实验误差定义为"零"因为已经把系统误差重新命名为"校准偏移"。

2. **形式化深度的严重不对称**：GraphTheoreticCharge中的3个`sorry`加上15个`postulate`，意味着论文的核心物理声明（连续极限、Einstein-Cartan方程、Chern-Simons识别、谱作用量）**全部**依赖于未证明的假设。这些不是边缘假设——它们是论文的物理核心。

3. **三级标注的必要性**：科学诚实要求区分：(a) 机器验证的证明，(b) 定义/概念引入，(c) 未证明的假设。当前表述将所有三类混为一谈，制造"全部已形式化"的虚假印象。

```
§5.4 Assessment of Formalization Completeness

The formalization effort in SYLVA spans approximately 8,000 lines of Lean 4
code across six modules. The algebraic infrastructure — graph definitions,
spectral properties, and combinatorial invariants — is machine-verified at
the [PROVED] level. These results constitute a reliable computational
foundation.

The physical derivations, however, are formalized predominantly at the
[ASSUMED] level. Fifteen axioms (or postulates) encode the core physical
content: the correspondence between graph degree and field strength, the
existence of a continuum limit, the Einstein-Cartan field equations, the
Chern-Simons topological identification, and the emergence of the Standard
Model gauge sector from the spectral action. None of these claims carries a
machine-verified proof. The "zero sorry" metric is technically correct —
these are declared axioms, not explicit sorry placeholders — but methodo-
logically, each axiom represents an unproven physical assumption equivalent
to a deferred proof obligation.

We classify the formalization depth as follows:
- Trivial: Direct translation of definitions into type theory; no substantive
  proof required (e.g., SpectralAction cutoff postulates).
- Superficial: Structure is formalized but key equivalences are asserted
  without proof (e.g., Chern-Simons level identification).
- Framework-level: Physical reasoning is encoded as a consistent axiom
  system, but the axioms themselves are not derived from more fundamental
  principles (e.g., Einstein-Cartan equations from network action).
- Research-level: Novel mathematical results with complete machine proofs.
  This category is currently empty for the physical derivation modules.

Honest assessment: SYLVA provides a formally consistent axiomatization of
an emergent-charge framework, not a proof that standard physics emerges
from network theory. The value of the formalization lies in making all
assumptions explicit and machine-readable, thereby enabling the community
to identify precisely which lemmas must be proved to elevate [ASSUMED] to
[PROVED] status.
```

## 修正任务3：Abstract和Introduction的声明强度

### 原文Abstract引用

> "offering a first-principles path toward the long-standing 'large-number puzzle'"
> "numerical simulations yield α ≈ 0.0073–0.008, achieving 5–6% agreement with the experimental value"

### 原文Introduction引用

> "we push this program to its logical extreme by proposing that charge itself... emerges from the connectivity structure"

### 修正理由

1. **"first-principles"的误用**：在物理学中，"first-principles"（从头算）有严格含义——指从最基本方程出发不引入经验参数的计算（如DFT中的从头算电子结构计算）。SYLVA模型引入了γ, C, κ等多个参数，且这些参数的值并非从更基本理论导出，因此不满足"first-principles"的标准定义。

2. **"emerges"的本体论承诺**："Charge emerges from connectivity"暗示了一种强涌现论立场——电荷作为实体完全由连接模式产生。但论文的证据仅支持一种更弱的声明：电荷的数值（耦合常数）与图的拓扑参数相关。"Emerges"需要替换为"is correlated with"或"may be structurally related to"。

3. **摘要-正文对应**：摘要声称"5-6% agreement"，但正文（修正后）显示这是参数敏感的结果， baseline的+0.7%具有偶然性。摘要必须反映这一不确定性。

## 修正任务4：§6.2 What Remains Open — 扩展Methodological Limitations

### 新增§6.3 Methodological Limitations

```
§6.3 Methodological Limitations

We conclude with an explicit enumeration of the methodological limitations
of the present work. This section is intended to assist reviewers and
readers in assessing the scope and appropriate context for our claims.

§6.3.1 Parameter Scan vs. Prediction

The numerical agreement between α_sim and α_exp reported in §4.2 is a
posterior correlation, not an a priori prediction. The model contains three
continuous input parameters (γ, C, κ) that are not fixed by internal
consistency or independent measurement. The baseline result (+0.7% error)
is one point in a five-point parameter scan; the tuned result (0.0% error)
is obtained by selecting the parameter combination that minimizes the
discrepancy. This procedure demonstrates the existence of a parameter
regime where the model reproduces the experimental value, but it does not
constrain the model because the parameter freedom is comparable to the
agreement achieved. A true prediction would require (i) a first-principles
calculation of γ, C, and κ from network combinatorics, or (ii) an
independent experimental determination of these parameters with which to
test the model's output for α.

We recommend that future work treat this as a parameter-sensitivity study
rather than a precision test. The physically meaningful result is the
systematic trend (higher γ → higher α_sim) and the existence of a broad
parameter regime where α_sim is within an order of magnitude of α_exp,
not the precise numerical value at any single parameter point.

§6.3.2 Formalization Depth

The formalization in Lean 4 covers approximately 8,000 lines of code. Of
the six modules, two (FiniteGraph and Utilities) operate at the
[PROVED] level with research-level mathematical content. The remaining
four modules (GraphTheoreticCharge, ContinuumLimit, EinsteinCartan,
ChernSimons, SpectralAction) formalize physical derivations predomi-
nantly at the trivial and superficial depth levels — definitions and
axiom declarations with minimal proof content. 

The "zero sorry" metric, while technically accurate, masks 15 unproven
axioms that encode the core physical claims. These axioms are not formal
analogues of physical laws (like conservation principles); they are
mathematical conjectures about the correspondence between discrete
structures and continuum field theories that remain to be proved. The
formalization is therefore best described as an explicit axiomatization
that makes assumptions machine-readable, not as a machine-verified proof
of physical emergence.

§6.3.3 Continuum Limit Rigour

The passage from the finite graph model (N = 2^20 nodes) to the continuum
theory relies on two postulates: (P1) the existence of a scaling limit in
which graph observables converge to smooth fields, and (P2) the identifi-
cation of this limit with Einstein-Cartan geometry. Neither postulate has
been proved. In particular, the question of whether a random graph ensem-
ble with the specified degree distribution almost surely admits a continuum
limit with the assumed geometric properties is open. The numerical results
are consistent with this assumption but do not constitute evidence for it.

Relatedly, the model uses a hard cutoff at N = 2^20 nodes with periodic
boundary conditions. The finite-size scaling behavior has not been studied,
and it is possible that the observed correlation between α_sim and α_exp
is a finite-size artifact that would disappear in the true N → ∞ limit.

§6.3.4 Chern-Simons Identification as Conjecture

The identification of the network topological invariant with the Chern-
Simons action (§3.4) is a conjecture, not a theorem. The formalization
encodes this identification as Axiom CS-2: "the graph characteristic
class equals the Chern-Simons form." No proof is provided that the
discrete combinatorial invariant satisfies the defining properties of
the Chern-Simons form (gauge invariance modulo integers, variation giving
the field equations, etc.). This is a significant gap because the Chern-
Simons identification underpins the claimed unification of gauge and
gravitational sectors.

§6.3.5 Appropriate Publication Context

Given the limitations enumerated above, we believe the appropriate
publication context for this work is:

Primary: arXiv preprint (hep-th or gr-qc), explicitly labeled as a
  "Research Programme" or "Framework Proposal" rather than a completed
  derivation.

Secondary: Conference proceedings (e.g., DICE, LOOPS, or similar
  quantum gravity meetings) where exploratory frameworks are presented
  with their open problems.

Not yet appropriate: Physical Review D as a regular article claiming
  derivation or prediction of the fine-structure constant. We do not
  believe the evidence standard for PRD — typically requiring either
  rigorous proofs or phenomenological predictions with controlled
  uncertainties — is currently met. We welcome editorial guidance on
  whether PRD's "Conceptual Papers" category might be suitable for a
  work that prioritizes formal axiomatization over physical prediction.
```

### 关键原则总结

1. **预测与后验拟合的区分**：如果一个参数需要调整到小数点后一位才能匹配数据，这是拟合，不是预测。诚实标注这一点不会削弱论文——它让读者知道模型的约束能力。

2. **已证明与已公理化的区分**：`axiom`和`postulate`在形式化系统中与`sorry`方法论等价——都代表"尚未证明"。声称"zero sorry"而隐藏15个axiom是技术性不诚实。

3. **涌现与相关的区分**："X emerges from Y"需要证明X的所有性质（包括动力学行为）都可由Y的结构决定。"X is correlated with Y"只需要统计证据。SYLVA的证据支持后者，而非前者。

4. **适宜投稿目标的诚实**：将研究纲领投稿到PRD作为常规文章，若被拒绝会浪费审稿资源并损害作者声誉。诚实评估论文的阶段，选择合适的投稿渠道，是科学成熟的标志。
