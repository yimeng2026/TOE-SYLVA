- **Benefits**: Conference travel, computing resources
- **Application**: [Apply here](fellowship_application.md)

- University of Waterloo (Perimeter Institute)

### Summer Schools

**Annual Sylva Summer School**
- **Location**: Rotates between partner institutions
- **Duration**: 2 weeks
- **Topics**: HERT, TSA, NTQ, computational methods
- **Target**: Advanced undergraduates and graduate students

### Undergraduate Research

**Sylva REU Program** (Research Experience for Undergraduates)

## 🤝 How to Join

### For Individual Researchers

1. **Join the community**
   - [Discord](https://discord.gg/sylva) for real-time chat
   - [Mailing lists](mailing_lists.md) for announcements
   - [GitHub](https://github.com/sylva-toe) for code contributions

2. **Attend events**
   - Weekly seminars (virtual)
   - Monthly workshops (hybrid)
   - Annual conference (in-person)

3. **Contribute**
   - Join a working group
   - Submit papers with Sylva framework
   - Mentor students

### For Institutions

1. **Review partnership options**
   - [Partnership types](partnership_types.md)
   - [Memorandum of Understanding template](mou_template.md)

2. **Contact coordination team**
   - Email: alliance@sylva-toe.org
   - Include: Institution profile, proposed collaboration, contact person

3. **Sign agreement**
   - Review and sign MOU
   - Appoint institutional liaison
   - Announce partnership

### For Students

1. **Learn Sylva**
   - Read the [Getting Started guide](../docs/getting-started/index.md)
   - Work through tutorials
   - Join student Discord channel

2. **Find opportunities**
   - Check [open positions](jobs.md)
   - Apply for fellowships
   - Join summer schools

3. **Connect with mentors**
   - Attend office hours
   - Participate in working groups
   - Reach out to researchers
